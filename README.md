# 📊 Global Superstore Sales Analysis (Power BI)

## 🔍 Objective

This project focuses on analyzing global retail sales data to identify:

* Profit drivers
* Customer value
* Revenue risk areas
* Growth trends

The goal is to move beyond static reporting and build a **decision-oriented dashboard**.

---

## 📁 Dataset

Global Superstore dataset (2012–2015)

---

## ⚙️ Dashboard Features

### 🟢 Core Metrics (Descriptive Layer)

* Total Sales: 12.68M
* Total Profit: 1.47M
* Profit Margin: 11.61%
* Total Orders & Customers

### 🔴 Advanced Metrics (Analytical Layer)

* YoY Growth %: 51.65
* Customer Lifetime Value (LTV): 728.23
* Revenue at Risk: 2.60M
* Return Rate & Loss Orders %
* Rolling 3-Month Sales Trend

---

## 📊 Key Visuals

* Sales by Category & Segment
* Market-wise distribution
* Profit by Sub-category
* Top 10 Customers
* Monthly Sales Trend
* Sales vs Rolling Performance

---

## 🧠 Key Insights

* Technology category drives highest revenue contribution
* Certain sub-categories show consistent low profitability
* Revenue concentration among top customers is high
* Detectable revenue risk segments across markets
